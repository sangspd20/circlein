Private vs. Public Circles

For private Circles, only members approved by the Account Owner can join if they receive a sharing link. You can share this link with people you trust such as family or close friends. Circledin will handle payments for private Circles, however the lowest price and on-time payments are not guaranteed.

A public Circle is listed on Circledin and any member can choose to join the plan.

Verified vs. Unverified

Your privacy is our main concern. To ensure maximum security and protection, Circledin is verifying our users. This status may affect the likelihood of a Circledin member joining your Circle, or an Account Owner accepting you into their Circle.

Top 3 Frequently Asked

Questions

What happens when someone in the family forgets to pay?

Circledin guarantees timely payments each month so that there are no disruptions to your mobile services.

What type of data does the Account Owner see?

Each of our Account Owners are verified and trusted Circledin community members. The Account Owner will only see high level usage data such as phone numbers, but not actual text messages, images, etc.

Are there any activation fees when I join?

Carriers do have activation fees, however some Circles can get their activation fees waived through special discounts. Activation fees are a one-time charge that typically ranges from $30-$40.
